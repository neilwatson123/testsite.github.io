Creutzfeldt-Jakob Disease (CJD)

This is a website dedicated to the rare, rapidly-progressive and universally fatal disease called CJD. CJD is a prion disease, in which brain cells rapidly deteriorate and die as toxic proteins misfold and accumulate in the brain. If you have any questions, please comment below.
